Likelihood

  1. rare
    It is quite improbable that the risk will occur.
  2. unlikely
    A low probability of occurring.
  3. possible
    Split odds.
  4. likely
    Probable.
  5. quite likely
    Quite probable.

Severity

  1. insignificant
    Negligible consequences
  2. minor
    Easily manageable consequences
  3. moderate
    Time consuming but manageable consequences
  4. major
    The consequences might be (a) negatively significant & extensively damaging, and (b) long-term.
  5. severe
    Detrimental, and probably irreversible, consequences.
